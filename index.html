DEADLINE

Entries must be submitted by Thursday, March 12th, 2020 — midnight into Friday. The 2020 Richard H. Driehaus Foundation Awards for Investigative Reporting are presented with the generous support of The Richard H. Driehaus Foundation. The Driehaus Foundation supports investigative reporting that fosters greater transparency, accountability, and effectiveness in government institutions at the local level.

ABOUT

The Better Government Association will present the Richard H. Driehaus Foundation Awards for Investigative Reporting in a virtual video ceremony at 4:30 p.m. Tuesday, July 14.

Judged by a rotating panel of journalists and media educators, the awards highlight the impact of Illinois investigative reporting as a reform tool within the context of state and local government waste, fraud and corruption.
